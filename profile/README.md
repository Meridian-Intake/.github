# Meridian Intake

**Private intake infrastructure for high-value service businesses.**

Meridian helps firms recover missed calls, qualify demand, route conversations, and produce inspectable intake evidence.

We work with service operators where missed inbound demand is expensive, time-sensitive, and operationally messy: law firms, clinics, dental practices, and premium home-service businesses.

---

## What Meridian Does

Meridian operates a controlled intake layer that helps businesses:

- Detect missed or delayed inbound calls
- Send approved SMS responses
- Qualify demand using structured questions
- Route conversations to the right person or team
- Log intake activity for review and accountability
- Produce reporting on missed-call recovery and intake performance

Meridian is not positioned as a generic chatbot, AI agency, or call centre replacement.

We build and operate intake-control systems for businesses that need clearer follow-up, stronger routing, better evidence, and less revenue leakage.

---

## Operating Principles

Meridian is built around five principles:

1. **Control before automation**  
   Intake systems should be supervised, bounded, and reviewable before they scale.

2. **Professional boundaries matter**  
   Meridian does not provide legal advice, medical advice, diagnosis, triage, or regulated professional judgment.

3. **Evidence beats hype**  
   Every deployment should produce inspectable logs, routing records, QA notes, and performance reporting.

4. **Privacy is operational, not decorative**  
   Intake systems should minimize unnecessary data collection and protect sensitive client information.

5. **Revenue recovery must be measurable**  
   Missed-call recovery, response speed, qualification quality, and escalation performance should be tracked clearly.

## What Meridian Does Not Do

Meridian does **not**:

- Give legal advice
- Give medical advice
- Diagnose, triage, or recommend treatment
- Replace licensed professionals
- Make final client acceptance decisions
- Operate as an unsupervised black-box chatbot
- Store secrets or sensitive client data in public repositories

---

## Repository Standards

Meridian repositories follow a controlled operating standard:

- Private-by-default for production systems
- Protected `main` branches
- Pull-request-based changes
- No secrets committed to code
- No client transcripts or sensitive intake data committed to GitHub
- Environment variables managed through secure secret stores
- Clear separation between development, staging, and production
- Documentation maintained alongside system changes
